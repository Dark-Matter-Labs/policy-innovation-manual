The Navigation
Kit

The central methodological resource for Task 6.5: a structured set of policy-mapping canvases supporting cities at different stages — from early challenge surfacing and prioritisation, through diagnostic mapping, into policy-mix development and evaluation. Cities can enter wherever they are and build through it over time.

These tools were developed iteratively through delivery — tested in real engagements and refined in response to what worked. They are not polished standalone products; they are working tools. The Kit has been the facilitation backbone across the Romanian studio, the Finnish lab, the Seasonal School and the online learning programme.

Group 01Challenge-framing tools

For helping cities define and bound their challenge space before committing to a deeper engagement. Particularly relevant for the integrated and light-touch tiers — they are what let a city adviser or CCC reviewer surface a real policy challenge rather than a surface-level frustration.

Canvas

Problem-behind-the-problem

A diagnostic for moving through the visible challenge to the structural one underneath it.

Canvas

Stakeholder & power mapping

Who holds power over the decision, where the veto points sit, and what the relational terrain looks like.

Matrix

Challenge prioritisation

Comparing candidate challenges against readiness, leverage and transferability.
