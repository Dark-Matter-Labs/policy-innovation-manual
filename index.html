Policy Innovation
User Manual

NetZeroCities' support for the EU Climate-Neutrality Mission shows how often cities are not short of ambition. What Mission implementation requires is the harder work of navigating, aligning and reshaping policy across every level of governance. This manual is a working record of how that work was done — the model, the methods, and the practice behind three years of it.

Where this comes fromThe EU Climate-Neutrality Mission

This User Manual presents the methods, tools and insights produced by Task 6.5 — Collaborative policy & regulatory dialogues and innovation — set up to enable collaboration across local, regional, national and European governance levels on policy.

The work was developed within NetZeroCities, as part of the agreement to support the EU's Climate-neutral Mission Cities. Its specific aim was to move beyond a shared diagnosis of the barriers to climate neutrality, toward a transformation of the governance and regulatory frameworks that cities, national actors and EU institutions work within. Regulatory Sandbox Deep · national approval

A controlled space to test new regulatory approaches in real conditions before formal adoption. The most resource-intensive and politically complex format — it cannot be advanced by NZC or city teams alone.
